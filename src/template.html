About Us

At Grow Your Ohana (GYO), we use the latest in modern internet technology to help Hawaiian businesses put customer acquisition on autopilot, letting you focus on running your business while we bring you an endless stream of customers.

GYO was founded by Ryan Combes (featured) - a Hawai`i-grown technologist, marketer, and entrepreneur who grew tired of seeing Hawaiian businesses technologically left in the dust. With a strong background in technology and marketing, he decided to put it to use, and now serves a number of Hawaiian businesses through GYO.
