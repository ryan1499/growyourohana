About Us

Grow Your Ohana (GYO) is a Hawai`i-based digital advertising agency that helps e-commerce brands increase their online sales. We use social media advertising to profitably acquire customers, letting you focus on running your business while we get you customers.

GYO was founded by Ryan Combes (featured) - a Hawai`i-grown tech enthusiast and entrepreneur, who realized Hawaiian companies were missing out on new technologies to grow their businesses. To solve this, he founded GYO and has since helped numerous brands take their online sales to the next level.
